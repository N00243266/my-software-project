WELCOME!

Welcome to my digital gallery. This space combines immersive 3D navigation, historical artworks, and personal artistic expression in one interactive experience. What makes it unique is the ability to walk through curated virtual rooms, encounter artworks in space, and experience painting not as a flat collection, but as a living exhibition.

Diana Kovika Digital Gallery

Move through the gallery using W, A, S, D and look around with your mouse. On mobile devices, use the left joystick to walk and drag on the right side of the screen to change your view.

Approach each artwork to reveal its title and description. The exhibition unfolds through movement — step closer to encounter each piece in full.
